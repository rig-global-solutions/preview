Hypothetical Case Study

CLIENT PROFILE

Priya Sharma

28-year-old Freelance UI/UX Designer

Background & Goals:

  • Fluctuating monthly income (₹80k - ₹1.2L).
  • Goals: Save for a down payment on a house (₹10L in 3 years), build a retirement fund, and optimize taxes as a freelancer.
  • Challenges: No structured savings plan, unsure about insurance needs, and unaware of her credit score.

Our Process in Action:

Onboarding: Priya provided her bank statements, ITRs, and completed our discovery questionnaire. We created her profile and set clear expectations.

Service Delivery: We analyzed her inconsistent cash flow and created a 'pay-yourself-first' system. We recommended a diversified portfolio of mutual funds (SIP) for her house goal, suggested NPS for retirement & tax savings, and identified a suitable term and health insurance plan.

Outcome: Priya now has a clear financial roadmap. She is consistently saving ~30% of her average monthly income, her insurance is in place, and she has a plan to improve her credit score from 720 to 780+ within a year. The monthly check-ins help her stay on track with her budget.
