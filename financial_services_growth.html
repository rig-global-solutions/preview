Phase 1: Discovery & Onboarding

Timeline: Week 1 | Goal: Establish a solid foundation and clear understanding of the client's business.

Initial Consultation

Understand goals and pain points with a standardized discovery questionnaire.

Needs Assessment & Proposal

Tailor the Growth Package scope and present a clear proposal and engagement letter.

KYC & Documentation

Collect all necessary documents via a secure portal using a clear checklist.

Onboarding Kick-off

Introduce the team, set expectations, and finalize the project plan in a formal meeting.

Phase 2: Strategy & System Setup

Timeline: Week 2-3 | Goal: Implement robust systems and create a strategic financial roadmap.

Diagnostic Review

Perform a deep-dive analysis of historical data to identify trends, risks, and opportunities.

Technology Setup

Set up or migrate the client to a cloud accounting platform (e.g., Zoho) and provide training.

Financial Roadmap Development

Collaborate with the client to build a 12-month financial plan, defining key budgets, KPIs, and growth targets.
