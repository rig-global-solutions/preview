Strategic Implementation Roadmap

Starter Package

A Framework for Excellence

This document provides a standardized, step-by-step process for executing the Business Strategy Starter Package. Its purpose is to ensure a consistent, high-quality, and impactful experience for our startup clients, from onboarding to delivery and beyond.

Vision, Mission & Goals

Defining the startup's core identity and direction.

Business Model Canvas

Architecting the commercial logic and value proposition.

SWOT & PESTEL Analysis

Assessing internal capabilities and external market forces.

Startup Strategy Advisory

Providing continuous expert guidance and mentorship.

Project Execution Timeline

Phase 1: Onboarding & Discovery (Week 1)

Objective:

To formally engage the client, understand their vision at a deep level, and establish a solid foundation for the strategic partnership.

  • Key Activity: Conduct Kick-off & Deep-Dive Session.
  • Tools: Client Questionnaire, Welcome Kit, Project Management Board (Trello/Asana).
  • Deliverable: Signed Service Agreement & Completed Discovery Document.

Phase 2: Foundational Framework (Weeks 2-3)

Objective:

To collaboratively define the core strategic pillars of the business through structured workshops.

  • Key Activity: Facilitate two workshops: (1) Vision, Mission & Goals, and (2) Business Model Canvas / Lean Canvas.
  • Tools: Miro/Mural for virtual whiteboarding, Canvas Templates.
  • Deliverable: Draft Vision/Mission Statements & Completed Business Model Canvas v1.

Phase 3: Analysis & Synthesis (Week 4)

Objective:

To enrich the foundational framework with data-driven insights from internal and external environmental analysis.

  • Key Activity: Conduct internal (SWOT) and external (PESTEL) analysis. Synthesize all findings.
  • Tools: Market Research Reports, Competitor Analysis Frameworks, Internal Document Templates.
  • Deliverable: "Foundational Strategy V1.0" draft document, including analysis summaries.

Phase 4: Delivery & Ongoing Advisory (Monthly Cycle)

Objective:

To deliver the initial strategy, establish a rhythm of continuous improvement, and provide ongoing expert guidance.

  • Key Activity: Present Strategy V1.0, establish monthly check-in calls, set up communication channels (Slack/Email).
  • Tools: Video Conferencing, Shared Calendar, Reporting Template.
  • Deliverable: Finalized Strategy Document, Monthly Action Plan, Continuous Advisory Support.

Phase 5: Follow-up & Growth (Quarterly)

Objective:

To review progress, adapt the strategy to new learnings, and identify opportunities to provide further value to the client.

  • Key Activity: Conduct Quarterly Business Review (QBR) to assess progress against goals and pivot where necessary.
  • Tools: QBR Presentation Template, Performance Dashboards.
  • Deliverable: QBR Summary Report & Identification of cross-selling opportunities (e.g., Virtual CFO, Fundraising).
